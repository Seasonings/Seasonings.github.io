AAEPC offers comprehensive consulting services

Ensure your agency or organization achieves full compliance to continue to receive Medicare and Medicaid funding and reimbursement.

Find Out More

AAEPC will provide your agency with emergency preparedness resources and training to promote patient preparedness and continuity of care

Policies and Procedures

Your agency will be equipped with a personalized Emergency Operations Plan and supplemental Emergency Action Plans for each type of hazard we identify.

Communications Plan

Your agency will be provided contact information to match emergency official at the local, county, and state level. We will implement a mass notification system to reach your staff instantaneously.

Training and Testing

You will receive emergency preparedness training and in-services. I will facilitate tabletop and functional exercises annually for your staff and provide you direction to participate in full-scale community exercises.

Hazard Vulnerability Analysis

Together we will conduct a facility-based and community-based risk assessment that will assist your agency in addressing the needs of their patient populations. We will consider hazards most likely to occur in the surrounding area, including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Man-made disasters,
  • Facility-based disasters;
  • Equipment and utility failures (power, water, gas, etc.);
  • Interruptions in communication, including cyber-attacks;
  • Loss of all or portion of a facility.
